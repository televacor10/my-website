Buddha Once Said:

  1. If you truly love yourself, you could never hurt another
  2. Hatred does not cease through hatred at any time.Hatred ceases through love.This is unalterable law.
  3. Happiness does not depend on what you have or who you are.It solely relies on what you think.
  4. It is ridiculous to think that somebody else can make you happy or unhappy.
  5. Happiness never decreases by being shared.
  6. Happiness is not having a lot but happiness is giving a lot
  7. If anything is worth doing, do it with all heart.
  8. Do not dwell in the past, do not dream of the future, concentrate the mind on the present moment.
  9. Have compassion for all beings, rich and poor alike; each has their suffering. Some suffer too much, others too little.
  10. An idea that is developed and put into action is more important than an idea that exist only as an idea
  11. One moment can change a day, one day can change a life and one life can change the world.
  12. Nothing ever exists entirely alone; everything is in relation to everything else.
  13. If the problem can be solved why worry? If the problem cannot be solved worrying will do you no good.
  14. In the sky, there is no distinction of east or west, people create distinctions out of their minds and then believe them to be truth.
  15. People with opinions just go around bothering one another.
  16. If you find no one to support you on the spiritual path, walk alone. There is no companionship with immature.
  17. Everything that has a beginning has an ending. make your peace with that and all will be well.
